Press A Key to Start

How to play

  • The screen will show you a random colour, when you press any key on your keyboard
  • You have to click on the same colour in order to proceed to next level
  • If you succeed,you'll enter level 2
  • The screen will again show you a random colour
  • This time, you have to press the first colour and this colour in the same pattern to proceed further
